About The Series

SurvivORGs is a series of Survivor ORGs run by staff and members of the Reddit Survivor Discord. It has currently completed six seasons, with a seventh in pre-production, information for which can be found above.

The aim of the series is to provide a decent emulation of the conditions of Survivor in real life, while remaining a fun and fair experience for those involved.
